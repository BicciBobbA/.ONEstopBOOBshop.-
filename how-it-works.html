How It Works

Sending clothing packs to your loved one in a correctional facility is simple and secure with The OneStopBOOBShop.

  1. Browse Packs: Choose the clothing care pack that best suits your loved one's needs.
  2. Place Your Order: Select a size, add to cart, and proceed through our quick checkout.
  3. We Handle the Rest: We package and ship the pack directly to the correctional facility with all the proper approvals.

No need to visit stores or worry about what’s allowed—everything we sell is facility-approved.
