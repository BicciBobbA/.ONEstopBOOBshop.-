How It Works

Sending clothing packs to your loved one is simple and secure:

  1. Browse Packs: Choose a pack for your loved one's needs.
  2. Select Size: Choose S, M, L, or XL.
  3. Checkout: Provide delivery info to the facility.
  4. We Ship: We send it directly and securely to them.

We take care of everything. No hassle, no mistakes, just care.
