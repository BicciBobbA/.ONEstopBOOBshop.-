Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers!

Here are some common questions about The OneStopBOOBShop and how we help you support your loved ones.

How long does delivery take?

We process orders within 24 hours. Delivery to correctional facilities typically takes 3-5 business days, depending on the location.

Are the clothing packs approved for prisons?

Yes! All our packs meet standard correctional facility guidelines across Australia.

What sizes are available?

All clothing items come in Small, Medium, Large, and Extra Large.

Can I customize a pack?

Currently, we offer pre-made packs to ensure compliance. Custom packs may be available in the future!

How do I pay for an order?

We are working on integrating online payments. For now, please contact us to arrange manual payment processing.

What if an item isn’t allowed by the facility?

We guarantee that all items are compliant. If an issue arises, we will work directly with the facility to resolve it.

Need more help?

Visit our Contact Page and we’ll be happy to assist you.
