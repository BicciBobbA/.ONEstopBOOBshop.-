How It Works

It's easy to send a clothing care pack:

  1. Choose a pack that suits your loved one's needs.
  2. Select their size (S–XL).
  3. Complete the order with delivery details.
  4. We handle the rest — shipping directly to the facility.

Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes are available?

We offer sizes S, M, L, and XL for all clothing items.

Can I send a pack to any correctional facility?

We ship to most correctional facilities in Australia. Please check with the facility for approved items if you're unsure.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3–5 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major debit/credit cards and PayPal.
