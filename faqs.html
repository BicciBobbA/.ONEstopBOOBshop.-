FAQs

Q: Are the care packs approved by correctional facilities?
A: Yes. We carefully select all items in our packs to meet common correctional facility guidelines. All clothing is basic, no-frills, and complies with typical regulations (no inappropriate logos or forbidden materials).

Q: What sizes are available for the clothing items?
A: Our packs are available in sizes S, M, L, and XL. You can choose the appropriate sizes for your loved one when placing an order. We ensure each item in the pack is the size you need.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: We offer quick delivery, typically within 3–5 business days. Once your order is confirmed, we immediately prepare the pack and ship it out as soon as possible.

Q: What information do I need to provide to place an order?
A: You will need to provide the inmate’s full name, their Correctional Reference Number (CRN) or inmate ID, and the name of the correctional facility where they are located. This information ensures the package is delivered correctly.

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Placing an order is simple. First, select the care pack you want and click the “Order Now” button. Then, fill out the order form with the required details. We’ll take it from there and handle the rest of the process.
