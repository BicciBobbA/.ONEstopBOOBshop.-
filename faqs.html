Common Questions

How long does delivery take?

All orders are processed within 24 hours and delivered to correctional facilities within 3-5 business days.

What sizes are available?

All clothing packs are available in Small, Medium, Large, and Extra Large.

Are these items approved for correctional facilities?

Yes, all items are selected based on standard prison regulations for clothing and accessories.

Can I customize a pack?

At this stage, only pre-made packs are available. Custom packs may be introduced soon!

What payment methods do you accept?

Currently, payments are handled manually. Please contact us for processing orders.
