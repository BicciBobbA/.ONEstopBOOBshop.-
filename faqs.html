Common Questions

What sizes are available?

All packs come in Small, Medium, Large, and Extra Large sizes. You’ll choose the size when you order.

How fast is delivery?

We typically deliver care packs to the facility within 3–5 business days after processing your order.

Can I customize the items in a pack?

Currently, all packs are pre-approved and pre-set to ensure fast approval by correctional facilities. Customization isn't available yet.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and most major debit cards via secure checkout.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is processed, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link.

Do you deliver to all correctional facilities?

We deliver to most Australian correctional facilities. If you are unsure, feel free to contact us.
